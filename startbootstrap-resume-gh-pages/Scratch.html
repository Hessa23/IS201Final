What is Vocal Performance?

Vocal performance is the act of singing for an audience. My experience with vocal performance with the most part has been with my time in showchoir. During my performances, we would sing and dance as a group or in solos for an audience as part of a showcase show.

Why am I passionate about singing?

Why am I passionate about singing? Because it brings me joy! Throughout my whole life, music has brought me comfort and has helped me to let out my emotions that sometimes I keep bottled up. Vocal performance has given me an outlet and a way for me to simply be myself.

What are some ways that you can gain experience and confidence in your performing abilities?

  1. Take classes from school or private teachers
    • If you're at a school, talk to the choir teacher about lessons or if they know any vocal coaches
    • Be involved in your school or community choir

    • Take extra lessons to improve your skills

    • Try new things with your voice under your coaches direction



  2. Attend as many auditions as you can
    • Auditions can be scary, but just like job interviews, you'll never know what good could happen unless you try
    • Auditions give you good practice with public speaking, performing, and preparation
    • Will allow you to meet people and grow your network
    • Will help you to learn baout future or new audition opportunities



  3. Take time to improve your musicality skills
    • Give your all in class/ at lessons and take what your learned and do it on your own later
    • Follow vocal warmups and style tricks on platforms like YouTube
    • Practice blending or creating harmonies to your favorite songs and artists
    • Remeber to have fun! Singing is about learning, growth, and most of all, just doing your best



  4. Get involved with others in the performing arts space
    • Make friends with the people in your performing group or at auditions
    • Becoming friends with people interested in the same space as you will help to motivate you to maintain your skills and get better
    • Building this network creates a support system for everyone in this space
